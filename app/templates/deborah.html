Deborah

Initially , Debora sought assistance from the HOLO organization, hoping to find a solution for her children’s care. However, recognising that separating Debora from her children would not provide a long term solution, our organization instead offered her enrollment in our tailaring program.

This training enabled Debora to acquire valuable skill, generating income and empowering her to provide for her twins. Our approach focuses on fostering self sufficiency, transforming individuals into productive members of their communities.

We take pride in Debora’s remarkable progress, as she has evolved into a skilled tailor, not only supporting her children, but also contributing to the community’s well being.
