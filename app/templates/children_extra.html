These are the stories of some of our children




Meet Annabel, a shining star in our organization. For 12 incredible years, Annabel has called our community home. Rescued at just 3 years old, she was found wandering village streets, searching for food for herself and her loyal dog. Even at a tender age, Annabel's aspirations knew no bounds – she dreamed of becoming a doctor. More...

Meet Benjamin, a bright and talented young boy who has been a part of our family for 8 incredible years. Abandoned at just 3 years old, Benjamin has grown into a confident and compassionate individual. More...

And here comes Abraham, a resilient and ambitious young man who has overcome unimaginable adversity. This young orphan suffered from untreated vision problems due to lack of access to medical care. By age three, he had developed a partial blindness in one eye. Abandoned at just 3.5 years old, Abraham was left to fend for himself, often sitting on his father’s grave, relying on the kindness of strangers for food. More...
