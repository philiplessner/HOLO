Children


Our organization serves children from infancy to young adulthood, with ages ranging from new-born to 18 years old. We currently care for 60 orphaned children. Of these, 45 are enrolled in school, while 15 younger children are waiting for their educational journey to begin.HOLO provides safe haven for vulnerable children from various backgrounds. We rescue street children, take in abandoned infants found near our premises, and welcome total orphans from nearby villages. Additionally, we work with local authorities to accept children from desperate young mothers who cannot afford to care for them, and are referred to us through the local police. Defying adversity, our young heroes are fueled by determination and passion. As orphans, they’ve faced unimaginable challenges, yet they remain steadfast in pursuing their dreams - Whether in academia, athletics, or the spotlight of the entertainment industry. Their resilience and spirit are a beacon of hope, inspiring us to believe in the power of the human spirit.
