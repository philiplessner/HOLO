Mission and Vision

Our mission is to provide comprehensive care and support to vulnerable children and widows, empowering them to reach their full potential. We offer education, counseling, medical aid, and basic necessities to orphaned, neglected, and abandoned children, while equipping widows with skills and resources to become self-sufficient and independent.



Our vision is to transform the lives of 6,000 African children and widows, breaking the cycle of poverty and dependence. Building on our success of over 100 children now leading independent lives, we are striving to create a brighter future where every individual has the opportunity to realize their God-given potential and contribute positively to their society. Through our holistic approach, we aim to create a ripple effect of hope, resilience, and empowerment that echoes across generations.
