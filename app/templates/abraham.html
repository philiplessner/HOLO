Abraham

Fortunately , our organization discovered Abraham and provided him with safe haven and support. When asked about his dreams, Abraham’s eyes sparkled with determination and shared his aspiration to become a skilled pilot. Which seemed improbable given his visual impairment.

Demonstrating faith in Abraham’s potential, our organization facilitated a comprehensive eye examination. The diagnosis revealed that Abraham’s condition could be corrected with a simple $2 eye drop treatment. Following a five- day regimen, Abraham’s vision was fully restored. We are delighted that the HOLO organization could provide timely intervention and support, transforming Abraham's life and reviving his dreams.

This experience underscores the significance of accessible healthcare and the profound impact it can have on individuals and communities. Fast forward 15 years, Abraham has blossomed into a brilliant, friendly, and driven individual. A straight A student, he’s currently thriving in high school, with his sights set on aviation school in 2027. We’re thrilled to witness Abraham’s growth and can't wait to see his dreams take flight!
