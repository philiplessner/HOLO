Donate




Despite the HOLO organization’s noteworthy achievements, our journey has not been without challenges. As we strive to fulfil our mission, we recognize the importance of collaborative partnership. We invite like-minded individuals and organizations to join us in bringing hope to those who need it most.

Sponsorship Opportunities

  1. Child educational sponsorship: $600 per child per year (or $50 per month).
  2. Sponsor an employee’s salary. Enabling us to maintain and expand our team: $200 per employee monthly.
  3. Widows Entrepreneurship sponsorship: One time gift of $200 per widow to start a small business.
  4. Vocational School support: Donate to purchase essential equipment, such as sewing machines. The Cost of one sewing machine is $170.
  5. Program-Specific sponsorship: Support initiatives like chicken farming for egg production.

Other Ways to Support Us

  1. Digital presence: Assist us in developing a website to share our stories, mission, and work.
  2. Awareness and outreach: Help us create promotional materials, such as flyers, to raise awareness about our organization.
  3. Guidance and expertise: Offer advice, education, and prayer support to enhance our operations and effectiveness.
  4. Partnership and Fundraising : Collaborate with us to achieve the vision and provide financial Support for the mission.

We also invite you to join us on the mission trip to Uganda where you can participate in our on-the -ground work. Together we can make a meaningful difference in the lives of those we serve.

Expanding Educational Opportunity and empowering communities.

To fulfil our mission of providing education and support to vulnerable children, HOLO organization aims to establish both elementary and trade schools. This initiative will offer free education to those in need, alleviating the financial burden of school tuition and enabling us to extend our support to a greater number of vulnerable children. To ensure the sustainability of our education program, we plan to open the school to non vulnerable students who will pay tuition fees. The revenue generated will be utilized to operate the schools and provide for the vulnerable children.

Infrastructure Development

Currently, HOLO organization possesses three acres of land, which we intend to expand by acquiring an additional three acres. This expansion will enable us to construct both the trade school and elementary school, providing a comprehensive education environment. Furthermore we plan to establish a farmer’s market, offering a dedicated space for widows to sell their fresh produce. This initiative will empower them to earn a steady income, supporting their livelihood and promoting economic independence.
